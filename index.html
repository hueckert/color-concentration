COLOR

CONCENTRATION

  1. Objective: Match pairs of identical card colores by remembering their positions and what's underneath.
  2. Player clicks and flips over two cards in one turn, trying to find a matching colors.
  3. Matching: If the card colors match, they remain flipped.
  4. Non-Matching: If the card colors don't match, they are flipped back over, player tries again.
  5. Game End: The game ends when all color pairs have been matched.
  6. Game continues until all matching card colors are found
